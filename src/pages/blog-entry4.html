Strategy Guide for Beating Operation Howling God in XCOM Chimera Squad

Dane Dwarka published on March 20, 2022

XCOM Chimera Squad is a turn-based video game released by Firaxis Games in 2020. It has a mission called Operation Howling God where you evacuate a VIP to the exit on your Armoured Personnel Carrier (APC.) This guide has tips for completing the mission on Impossible difficulty.

You should choose this squad for the mission:

Squad Member Weapon Utility Item Rank
Cherub Pistol Flashbang Grenade Field Agent
Zephyr Pangolin Gauntlets Cease Fire Grenade Special Agent
Godmother Enhanced Shotgun A.P. Rounds Special Agent
Axiom Enhanced Shotgun Mobile Inducer Special Agent

The mission starts with the breach phase.. Make your squad breach in the following order:

  1. Cherub
  2. Zephyr
  3. Godmother
  4. Axion

Use Cherub’s Phalanx ability while breaching to prevent damage to your squad.

Your squad deploys to the bottom of the map with your APC on the top of the map.

Move Cherub and the VIP along the bottom/right side of the map taking cover behind the barricades and burnt out cars. Cherub must use his Kinetic Shield to protect the VIP.

Move Godmother along the same path as Cherub. You must eventually move her into the middle of the map.

Move Zephyr and Axion along the left/top side of the map taking cover where possible.

Move the VIP to the top right hand corner behind the flat bed truck as in this diagram:

Dash the VIP to the exit when possible. Ending the VIP’s turn on the exit lets you evacuate them immediately. The mission objective is satisfied when the VIP is evacuated.

The following lists one or more actions that your squad members should take on their turn.

Zephyr should:

  • Use Parry after killing someone.
  • Use Momentum to kill someone and then dash to the exit.
  • Use Momentum to kill a flanking enemy and then take cover.
  • Use the ceasefire grenade on enemies.

Axiom should:

  • Use Psych Up at the start of his turn.
  • Smash enemies if he is out of ammunition.
  • Use the mobile inducer to move another squad member into a good position.

Cherub should:

  • Use Kinetic Shield to protect the VIP. If the VIP is unavailable then use it to protect someone else.
  • Use Charged Bash if he is out of ammunition and can’t reload his pistol.
  • Use the flashbang grenade to protect squad members from enemies.

Godmother should:

  • Outflank enemies and attack them.

All squad members should:

  • Try to kill or wound an enemy each turn.
  • Move slowly toward the exit.
  • Dash for the exit when possible. Evacuate them when they land on the exit.

You cannot kill all the enemies on this map because of their perpetual reinforcements. You must evacuate as much of your squad as possible.

I hope this guide helps you beat Operation Howling God.
