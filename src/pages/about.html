My name is Dane Dwarka. I was born in Trinidad, lived in Ottawa for a few years, and then moved to Winnipeg. I have lived in Winnipeg for most of my life.

I graduated from Red River College (now called Red River College Polytechnic) in 2005 with honours from the Computer Analyst/Programmer Diploma program. I worked as a computer programmer for several years. I have recently enrolled in the Manitoba Institute of Trades & Technology’s Software Developer program to upgrade my skills.

I like to play chess. I have a membership at chess.com which is an online chess community.

I also do some cooking. My ethnic background is West Indian and I’ve been trying to learn that cuisine.
