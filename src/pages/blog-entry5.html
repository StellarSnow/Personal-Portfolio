My Samsung Galaxy S7 Still Works Just Fine

Dane Dwarka published on April 15, 2022

I bought my Samsung Galaxy S7 (S7) when it was released in spring of 2016. I have had it for 6 years and it still works great. This blog posts covers the current strengths and weaknesses of my phone.

Battery Life

If I use my phone for a few phone calls, a couple of Internet searches, watching YouTube, etc. then I can get 2 days of battery life from it. If I use my phone excessively then it lasts for 1 day.

Operating System

The S7 runs the Android operating system. It was launched with Android version 6 and was upgraded to Android version 8. Samsung stopped updating the operating system in 2020. The S7 is still useful with Android version 8. Androidâ€™s current version is 12.

Useful Apps

Many apps still work even though my phone is 6 years old and has an outdated operating system.

Here are the apps I use:

  • Facebook
  • YouTube
  • Instagram
  • Samsung Internet
  • Samsung Music
  • Camera
  • Voice Recorder
  • Weather widget
  • Google Maps
  • WhatsApp
  • Microsoft Word
  • Email

These apps work as effectively as the first day I bought my phone.

Internal Storage

My S7 came with 32 GB of internal storage. System/User data takes up 22 GB of space. I had a 64 GB SD memory card installed to gain more space. Pictures, videos, music, and documents are now stored on the SD card.

Conclusion

My Samsung Galaxy S7 is 6 years old and still going strong. Although the battery may only last a day, my phone still works well for me. I can post on Facebook, watch and comment on YouTube videos, find places with Google Maps, use email, etc. I plan to keep my phone for at least another year.
